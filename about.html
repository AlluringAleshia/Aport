About Me

Software Developer & UI/UX Designer

I'm Aleshia McRae, a Full Stack Web Developer, UI/UX Designer, and a recent Computer Information Systems graduate from Florida A&M University. I aspire to build web applications that are both highly functional and user-friendly. I am skilled in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Spring Boot, and C#/.NET 8.0, with a genuine love for crafting clean interfaces that seamlessly integrate beautiful design with an intuitive User Experience.

With my university coursework and collaborative projects, I have worked on real-world applications that have allowed me to master how frontend design and backend engineering bridge together. During my roles as a Project Manager and Lead Developer, I have guided teams from initial concept to final deployment. My hands-on experience spans designing responsive layouts, building persistent database APIs, and creating interactive HUD systems in Unity

I am highly interested in the intersection of modern web development and Artificial Intelligence. This passion is reflected in my favorite projects, including Sonara, a full-stack meditation web app built with Spring Boot and ChatAI, a conversational agent built on .NET 8.0 using the OpenAI API. For ChatAI, I implemented context management and sentiment analysis to ensure the AI responds to users with intelligent awareness and human-like empathy.

My professional objective is to secure a web development or UI/UX position or internship where I can deliver tangible business value, apply my creative problem-solving and technical skills, and to continue growing alongside an innovative team.
